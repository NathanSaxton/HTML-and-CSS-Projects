Anchorage Jailbirds

Established in 1949

Anchorage is a quaint little town on the coast of Alaska, which in this universe is known for having the most fugitives on the run per capita. After "former" convict Muhammad Empanada moved to Alaska to escape his mundane jail cell, Empanada began to form what is now known today as the Anchorage Jailbirds. The Jailbirds have won only a single MLS cup in 1965.
